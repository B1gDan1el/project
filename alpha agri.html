🌿 What is Smart Farming?

Smart farming, also known as precision agriculture, integrates advanced technologies like IoT, AI, drones, and automation to optimize every aspect of farming.

  • ✅ Monitor soil health in real-time
  • ✅ Automate irrigation for water efficiency
  • ✅ Utilize drones for crop surveillance and spraying
  • ✅ Leverage AI-driven analytics for better decision-making
  • ✅ Reduce waste and maximize crop yields
